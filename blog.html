About

Meesho is an Indian social commerce platform that enables small businesses and individuals to start their online stores via social media channels. Founded in 2015 by Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal, Meesho empowers entrepreneurs, especially women, by offering a wide range of affordable fashion, home, and lifestyle products for resale across India.

Career

Careers at Meesho are driven by innovation, inclusivity, and digital empowerment. As one of India’s leading e-commerce platforms, Meesho offers diverse career opportunities in technology, product development, design, marketing, and operations.

Meesho fosters a vibrant and supportive culture, where every team member is encouraged to think big, take ownership, and contribute to building a platform that supports millions of entrepreneurs.

Why work at Meesho?

  • Empowering small businesses across India
  • Inclusive and growth-driven work environment
  • Opportunities in cutting-edge technology and e-commerce
  • Strong emphasis on diversity and innovation
  • Open culture that values every voice

Education

Vidit Aatrey and Sanjiv Barnwal, the co-founders of Meesho, are alumni of the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi.

Vidit studied at IIT Delhi with a focus on engineering and business problem-solving, while Sanjiv specialized in technology and product development. Their strong academic foundation played a crucial role in shaping Meesho's tech-driven platform and its user-first approach.

Family

At Meesho, the term "family" extends beyond the founders to include a diverse and passionate team of engineers, designers, marketers, and support staff. Co-founders Vidit Aatrey and Sanjiv Barnwal foster a culture that values balance between work and personal life.

Meesho's employee-first philosophy reflects in its inclusive policies, wellness initiatives, and celebrations that bring the team together like a close-knit family. This sense of belonging fuels innovation and builds a workplace where everyone feels respected and empowered to grow.
